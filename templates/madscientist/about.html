About MadScientist

It's ma baby! Don't say nothing bad about ma baby!

I wrote MadScientist to solve my own problem: I get a lot of different ideas every day for projects in many different areas, many more than I will probably ever have the time or inclination to do. I'm a person who sees a cool hand-lettered sign on the street and goes, "I should learn calligraphy!"

When I see something of someone else's that directly inspires me, Pinterest is the perfect organization tool, from online tutorials to styles I admire. But what about the projects that aren't on the internet yet? What about the ideas that aren't meant to be of any use or interest to anyone but myself?

Where do I put weird ideas for bands I'll never start, a party I want to have in 10 years, costumes that are way out of my budget, or hilarious Engrish phrases I never want to forget? From those questions MadScientist was born.

I currently have six different groups on my account: Create Me (Stuff to Make Someday...Maybe), Music Wishlist, Art Prompts and Techniques, Cool Phrases, Coding Projects, and Quotes. You can find out more about me here, if you like.
